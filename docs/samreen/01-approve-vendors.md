# How to Approve Vendors

Hi Samreen. This is how you approve vendors. Do this once a day.

## Step 1. Open the approvals page

Click this link: https://cthalaal.co.za/admin/applications

Look for a page that says **Applications** at the top.

## Step 2. Turn on the Ready to Approve filter

Look at the top of the page for a row of filter buttons.

Click the one that says **Ready to Approve**.

The list should get shorter. Only vendors who are ready will show now.

## Step 3. Pick the first 10 vendors

Each vendor has a little box on the left side (a checkbox).

Click the box next to the first 10 vendors in the list.

If there are fewer than 10, just pick all of them.

## Step 4. Click the green Approve all button

Look at the top right of the list for a green button that says **Approve all** or **Approve selected**.

Click it.

## Step 5. Confirm

A little pop-up box will ask "Are you sure?"

Click **Yes** or **Confirm**.

Wait until you see a green message that says "Approved".

## Step 6. If something goes wrong

If a vendor shows a red error message, do not panic.

Write down the vendor's name and what the red message says.

Skip that vendor and keep going with the rest.
