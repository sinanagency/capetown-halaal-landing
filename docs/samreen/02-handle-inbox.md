# How to Reply to Vendor Messages

Hi Samreen. This is how you reply to vendors who messaged us. Do this twice a day.

## Step 1. Open the inbox

Click this link: https://cthalaal.co.za/admin/bot-inbox

Look for a page that says **Inbox** at the top.

## Step 2. Turn on the Needs you filter

Look at the top of the page for a row of filter buttons.

Click the one that says **Needs you**.

Now only the messages that need a human reply will show.

## Step 3. Click the first message

The list shows one row for each vendor.

Click the first row at the top of the list.

The chat will open on the right side of the page.

## Step 4. Read what the vendor said

Read the last message from the vendor (the one on the left side of the chat).

Make sure you understand what they are asking.

## Step 5. Pick a reply

You have two ways to reply.

**Way A. Use a ready template.** Look for a button or dropdown that says **Templates** or **Pick a template**. Click it. Pick the one that matches the question. The reply text will fill in for you.

**Way B. Type your own.** If no template fits, click in the text box at the bottom and type a short reply yourself. Keep it friendly and short.

## Step 6. Click Send

Look for a blue button that says **Send** on the right of the text box.

Click it.

Wait until you see the message show up at the bottom of the chat.

## Step 7. Mark the thread as Done

Look for a button at the top of the chat that says **Mark Done** or **Done**.

Click it. The thread will disappear from the Needs you list. That is good.

## Step 8. Go to the next thread

Go back to the list and click the next thread at the top.

Repeat steps 4 to 7 until the Needs you list is empty.
