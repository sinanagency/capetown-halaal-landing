# How to Send a Mass Message to Vendors

Hi Samreen. This is how you send the same message to many vendors at once. Only do this when I ask you to.

## Step 1. Open the broadcast page

Click this link: https://cthalaal.co.za/admin/broadcast

Look for a page that says **Broadcast** at the top.

## Step 2. Pick the Sector

Look for a dropdown box labelled **Sector**.

Click it. A list will drop down (Food, Clothing, Services, etc).

Click the sector I told you to message.

## Step 3. Turn on the Has paid filter

Look for a toggle or checkbox that says **Has paid**.

Click it on. It should turn green or look switched on.

Now only vendors who paid will be in the list.

## Step 4. Check the count

Look at the top of the page for a number that says how many vendors will get the message (something like "Sending to 47 vendors").

Make sure the number looks right. If it is zero, double-check the Sector and Has paid filter in steps 2 and 3.

## Step 5. Pick the channel

You will see two buttons: **Mail** and **WhatsApp**.

Click the one I told you to use.

If I said "send by mail", click **Mail**.

If I said "send by WhatsApp", click **WhatsApp**.

## Step 6. Pick the template

Look for a dropdown labelled **Template**.

Click it. Pick the template name I gave you (for example, **Payment reminder** or **Setup day info**).

The message text will show below so you can read it.

## Step 7. Read the message one time

Read the full message in the preview box.

Make sure it looks right. Check the date, the time, the address.

If anything looks wrong, STOP and message me before you send.

## Step 8. Click Send

Look for a red button at the bottom that says **Send broadcast** or **Send**.

Click it.

A pop-up will ask "Are you sure? This will message X vendors."

Click **Yes** or **Confirm**.

## Step 9. Wait for the green tick

Wait until you see a green tick or a message that says "Sent to X vendors".

Do not close the page until you see it.

---

## Send back to me

When you finish, send me one message with:

1. The Sector you picked.
2. The channel you used (Mail or WhatsApp).
3. The template name.
4. How many vendors got the message (the final number).
5. Any red error message you saw.

Thank you Samreen.
