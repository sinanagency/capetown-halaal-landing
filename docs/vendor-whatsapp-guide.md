# How to manage your stall — the simple guide

You have **two ways** to manage your Cape Town Halaal Festival stall. Use whichever is easier.

## Way 1: WhatsApp (fastest)
Just send a message to the festival number. Type it like you would talk. Examples:

- **See where you stand:** type `status`
- **Get your invoice:** type `send my invoice`
- **Check your documents:** type `what documents do I need`
- **Change your details:** type `update my website to www.yourshop.co.za`
  (also works for `tagline`, `description`, `instagram`, `facebook`)
- **Pay your stall fee:** type `pay` (you get a safe link to pay)
- **Talk to the team:** type `note for team: I need an extra table`
- **See everything you can do:** type `help`

That is it. No login, no password. The bot already knows you from your phone number, and it only ever shows **your own** stall, never anyone else's.

## Way 2: Your online portal (full view)
For the detailed view (documents, marketing images, staff badges, contract):

1. Open **cthalaal.co.za/exhibitor/login**
2. Log in with **your email** and the password from your approval message
3. Everything is in the menu on the left

## When you get approved
The moment your stall is approved, you get a WhatsApp telling you these two ways and how to start. Reply `help` anytime.
