# Set up Samreen's Meta Business Portfolio for Cape Town Halaal — dead-simple checklist

> Goal: a brand-new **Meta Business Portfolio** (the thing Facebook used to call "Business Manager") owned by Samreen, holding CTH's pages + ad accounts, **verified** with the bank statement + business registration.
> Why: unlocks CTH's own FB/IG page roles, ad account, blue verified badge, catalog/commerce, and lifts ad spend limits.
> Time: ~15 min of clicking + 1–5 business days waiting for Meta to review the docs.
> Rule: this is **Samreen's portfolio, in Samreen's name**, separate from Nisria's. Nisria's portfolio stays as-is for WhatsApp.

---

## Before you start — have these ready (do not skip)

- [ ] Samreen's **Facebook personal account** logged in (same browser). She must be the one whose name is at the top right.
- [ ] **Business registration certificate** (the official PDF from CIPC or whichever registrar — NOT a screenshot, NOT a photo, the real cert). The legal name on it is what we type everywhere.
- [ ] **Bank statement** dated within the **last 90 days**, showing CTH's legal name and address. PDF preferred.
- [ ] A **work email** on CTH's domain if possible (e.g. `samreen@capetownhalaal.co.za`). Personal Gmail works too but verified domain email is stronger.
- [ ] CTH's **website URL** + **business phone** + **physical address** (must match what's on the registration cert + bank statement — typos here are why verifications fail).

> **Copy the legal name off the registration cert into a sticky note now.** Every field below that says "Legal business name" must be a character-for-character match. One missing "(Pty) Ltd" or extra space = rejected.

---

## Step 1 — Create the new Business Portfolio  (~3 min)

1. Open **https://business.facebook.com/overview** in Samreen's browser.
2. Top-left, click the **business switcher** (it shows the current portfolio name + avatar). Click **+ Create a business portfolio**.
   - If she's never had one, the page itself will show a big **Create account** button — same thing.
3. Fill in:
   - **Business and account name:** the legal name from the cert (e.g. `Cape Town Halaal (Pty) Ltd`).
   - **Your name:** Samreen's full name.
   - **Business email:** her work email.
4. Click **Next** → fill business details (address, phone, website). All must match the registration cert + bank statement.
5. Click **Submit**. ✅ Portfolio exists.
   - *Screen cue:* you land on Business Settings with an empty left menu (no pages/ad accounts yet).

---

## Step 2 — Confirm her email  (~1 min)

1. Open the work email inbox.
2. Click the **Confirm** link in the Meta email.
3. Back on business.facebook.com, refresh. The yellow "confirm email" banner should disappear.

---

## Step 3 — Add CTH's Facebook + Instagram pages  (~3 min)

> Only do this if CTH already has a Facebook page or Instagram business account. Skip if not — we can add them after verification.

1. Open **https://business.facebook.com/settings/pages**.
2. Top-right, click **Add → Add a Page** (use this if the page already exists and Samreen is its admin) **OR** **Claim a Page** (if she needs to take ownership).
3. Search the page name → **Add**.
4. Repeat for Instagram at **https://business.facebook.com/settings/instagram-accounts** → **Add → Connect your Instagram account** → log into IG.

---

## Step 4 — Open the Security Center / Business Verification  (~1 min)

1. Open **https://business.facebook.com/settings/security**.
   - If that URL bounces, go to **Business Settings → Security Center** from the left menu.
2. Find the card titled **Business Verification** → click **Start verification**.
   - *Screen cue:* a multi-step modal opens, "Verify your business".

---

## Step 5 — Fill the business details (must match docs!)  (~3 min)

In the verification modal:

1. **Legal business name:** paste the exact name from the registration cert (with the same punctuation, the same "(Pty) Ltd" suffix).
2. **Country:** South Africa.
3. **Street address:** match what's on the bank statement.
4. **City / Province / Postcode:** same.
5. **Business phone:** a number that can take an SMS or voice call from Meta later (they sometimes call to verify).
6. **Website:** CTH's live website URL (with `https://`).
7. **Business email:** the same work email from Step 1.
8. Click **Next**.

> **The #1 reason verifications fail is name/address mismatch.** If the bank statement spells it `Cape Town Halaal Pty Ltd` but the cert says `Cape Town Halaal (Pty) Ltd`, type the **cert version** here. We'll override with a doc explanation if Meta pushes back.

---

## Step 6 — Upload the documents  (~2 min)

Meta will ask for **at least one** doc that proves the legal name + at least one that proves the address. Bank statement + registration cert covers both.

1. **Document 1 — Business registration:**
   - Type: **Business license / Certificate of incorporation** (pick whichever option matches CIPC's cert — usually "Certificate of incorporation").
   - Upload the PDF.
2. **Document 2 — Bank statement:**
   - Type: **Bank statement**.
   - Upload the PDF.
3. **Make sure:**
   - Files are **PDF** (preferred) or clear PNG/JPG.
   - Files are **unedited** — no annotations, no highlighter marks, no redactions over the legal name or address.
   - Dates are **within 90 days** on the bank statement.
4. Click **Next**.

---

## Step 7 — Pick the verification method  (~1 min)

Meta will offer 1–3 of:
- **Email** to a domain-matching address (e.g. `info@capetownhalaal.co.za`).
- **SMS** to the business phone you entered.
- **Phone call** to that number.

Pick whichever Samreen can answer in the next 24h. **Email on the matching domain is the most reliable.**

1. Pick the method → **Send code**.
2. Wait for the code → type it in → **Submit**.
3. Final screen: "Your verification is being reviewed." ✅

---

## Step 8 — What happens next

- Meta reviews in **1–5 business days** (sometimes hours, sometimes longer).
- Status shows in **https://business.facebook.com/settings/security** → "Business Verification: Pending / Verified / Rejected".
- If **Verified** → the portfolio gets a green check; ad spend limits lift; she can claim a verified badge for the FB/IG page later.
- If **Rejected** → Meta tells you which field didn't match. Fix it (usually the legal name or address), and click **Resubmit**.

## Things that get verifications rejected (avoid these)

- Document is a **photo of a printout** instead of the original PDF.
- Bank statement is **older than 90 days**.
- Legal name on the form says `Cape Town Halaal` but the cert says `Cape Town Halaal (Pty) Ltd` — the suffix matters.
- Address line typed as `123 Main St` but the bank statement says `123 Main Street` — spell it out the way the bank does.
- Uploading the **VAT cert** instead of the **CIPC registration cert** (both are official, but Meta wants the registration cert specifically).
- Sharing a **Gmail/Hotmail business email** AND the docs are issued to a different name — match where possible.
